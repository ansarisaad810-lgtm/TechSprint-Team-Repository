# Future Scope

1. **Push Notifications:** Alert students when issues are resolved or materials updated.  
2. **AI Analytics:** Predict attendance trends or identify frequent campus issues.  
3. **Offline Mode:** Enable downloading study materials for offline use.  
4. **QR-Based Attendance:** Faster faculty attendance marking using QR codes.  
5. **Enhanced Security:** End-to-end encryption for sensitive media uploads.  
6. **Multi-College Support:** Expand app to multiple campuses with role-based access.
